# LearnSmart AI

LearnSmart AI is a responsible AI tool designed to help students understand confusing assignment directions without giving them answers or completing the work for them.

## What the App Does

LearnSmart AI helps students by:

* simplifying assignment instructions
* breaking tasks into clear steps
* explaining academic words such as "analyze," "compare," "justify," and "summarize"
* identifying what needs to be turned in
* helping students start their work independently

## What the App Does Not Do

LearnSmart AI is not designed to:

* write essays for students
* solve homework problems
* give final answers
* complete assignments
* replace teachers or student thinking

## Why I Built This

Many students get stuck before they even begin an assignment because the directions are long, confusing, or written in academic language. LearnSmart AI helps students understand what they are being asked to do while still requiring them to do their own work.

## Responsible AI Features

LearnSmart AI includes academic integrity guardrails. If a student asks the app to write an answer, complete the assignment, or do the work for them, the app redirects the student back to understanding the task, planning their work, or asking a teacher for help.

## Privacy

LearnSmart AI is designed to work without collecting unnecessary personal information. Students should not enter names, grades, addresses, or private school information.

## Technology

The project uses a web-based interface and AI-assisted instruction processing. The app takes assignment directions as input and returns a structured response with simplified directions, step-by-step guidance, key academic words, and a turn-in checklist.
