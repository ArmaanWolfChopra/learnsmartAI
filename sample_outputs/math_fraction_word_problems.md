# LearnSmart AI Sample Output: Math Assignment

## Assignment Input

Solve these 4 fraction word problems. Show your work for each problem, circle your final answers, and explain one of your answers in a complete sentence. Turn in the worksheet tomorrow.

## LearnSmart Output

### 1. What this assignment is asking

Complete 4 fraction word problems. Show how you solved each one, circle each final answer, and write one sentence explaining one of the problems.

### 2. Step-by-Step Checklist

- Read all 4 problems carefully.
- Solve each problem.
- Show your work for each problem.
- Circle each final answer.
- Choose 1 problem to explain.
- Write a complete sentence explaining how you solved it.
- Check that the worksheet is ready for tomorrow.

### 3. Academic Words

- **show your work** = write the steps you used to solve it
- **final answer** = the completed answer after solving
- **complete sentence** = a full sentence with a capital letter and period
- **explain** = tell how or why you did something

### 4. What to Turn In

The completed worksheet with work shown, final answers circled, and one written explanation.

### Responsible AI Note

LearnSmart AI can clarify the directions, but you still solve the math yourself.
