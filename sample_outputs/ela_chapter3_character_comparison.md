# LearnSmart AI Sample Output: ELA Assignment

## Assignment Input

Read Chapter 3 and write a paragraph comparing the main character at the beginning and end of the chapter. Use at least two details from the text. Turn in your paragraph by Friday.

## LearnSmart Output

### 1. What this assignment is asking

Read Chapter 3 and write one paragraph explaining how the main character changes from the beginning to the end. Use 2 details from the chapter.

### 2. Step-by-Step Checklist

- Read Chapter 3.
- Find how the character is at the start.
- Find how the character is at the end.
- Choose 2 details from the chapter.
- Write one paragraph explaining the change.
- Check that your paragraph is ready by Friday.

### 3. Academic Words

- **compare** = tell how two things are similar or different
- **details** = facts or examples from the text
- **main character** = the most important person in the story

### 4. What to Turn In

One paragraph by Friday.

### Responsible AI Note

LearnSmart AI helps explain assignments, but it does not write the answer for you.
